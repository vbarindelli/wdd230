Fall inlove with Andalucia

Andalucia is a region in the southern part of Spain. Well known for being a part of "la Costa del Sol" (the Coast of the Sun), its vibrant cultural activities and delicious food. It limits with Portugal to the west and with the Mediterranean sea to the south.
